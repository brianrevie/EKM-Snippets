Brackets Extention

EKM Ecommerce Tags can now be dynamically called through the brackets interface. quickly insert EKM tags into your project with highly efficient short codes and key commands. This will really speed up your workflow.

Extension is available in theBrackets extension registry.

Usage

Icon on the right toolbar shows you what snippets are available. Click any snippet to begin inserting. Ctrl-Alt-Space to insert the snippet. (Cmd-Alt-Space on Mac).

type a trigger followed by parameters (optional), then hit Ctrl-Alt-V or simply use the tag explorer and insert your tag this way!
